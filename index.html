Message from our Principal

Welcome to the best school in the Eastern Cape! Here, at TA, we strive to give you the best high school experience possible. We provide many activities in sport, culture, social, abd academics. It is, however, your responsibility to make it happen. YOU need to participate in all acivities provided. YOU need to embrace the TA experience and make it your own.

At Tomorrow Academy, appearance, respect for others, loyalty and punctuality are values that we treasure. Please ensure that you are always dressed according to the dress code, and that you look like an ambassador for our school. We always greet visitors, teachers, other staff members and fellow learners to show respect. We never talk ill of our school and we report problems to the appropriate staff member to be addressed accordingly. We are punctual at every activity.

For your five year career at TA, you have the opportunity to grow, learn and participate in a safe environement. We are striving to make your experience at TA, an unforgettable one.

Strength doesnt come from what you do. It comes from overcoming the things that you once thought you couldnt. At Ta, we are going to challenge you to do better academically and socially, and participate in sports and culture. Dream big and fulfil your TA dream. Learn from your teachers and seniors and be apart of the best.

Welcome to TA! May it be the best experience of your life.
